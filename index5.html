Our history

Snackify restaurant is the creation of Chef Rashmi. Having been a top chef for several years in the hotel industry, her passion to bring forth the rich and tasteful delights to the public drew her to start up her own business. What started in 2015 as a small restaurant, “Snackify” is now an established and well known name among everyone.

With a dedicated and friendly staff of over 50 men and women, we strive to provide the best quality food and service always. In keeping up with technological developments and consumer demand, we have been continually modernising our processes and provide computer based systems and online services.

Comprising of a skilled and artistic staff, we accept orders for wedding structures, design cakes, shape cakes and even customized & personalized sponge creations. Our menu currently consists of a diverse selection from sweets, cakes & savories to burgers, rice & noodles among many others.

Our dynamic catering team is equipped and ready to server any occasion. With the flexibility to provide you with a customized menu, we ensure our service blends smoothly with any event.

About Us

At Snackify restaurant, we love good food and great times. Our chefs use fresh, local ingredients to create delicious dishes. We're all about sharing tasty moments and creating a cozy place for you to enjoy. Come join us in savoring the simple joy of good eats and good company.
